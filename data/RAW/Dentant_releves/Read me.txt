Main columns of interest for you :

-Id_station = unique Id for a relevé

-Taxon_reference = official plant names

-Strata :
Herb = cover of any plant that is a not a tree, a shrub or a bush
inf_1m = cover of any ligneous plant smaller than 1 m
1a4m = cover of any ligneous plant between 1 and 4 m high
Sup_4m = cover of any ligneous plant taller than 4 m

-Programme = serveral relevés have been tagged because of specific programs. The relevés from Seb and me in high altitude are tagged "Ecologie verticale".

-L93X, L93Y: coordinates in Lambert 93 (EPSG : 2154)
